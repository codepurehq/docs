### üìù Step 2: Fill in the Integration Form

Complete the form using your GitLab account details:

| Field                | Description                                                                                                         |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Access Token**     | A [GitLab Personal Access Token](https://gitlab.com/-/profile/personal_access_tokens) with appropriate permissions. |
| **GitLab URL**       | Use `https://gitlab.com` for GitLab.com, or your GitLab instance URL if self-hosted.                                |
| **Integration Name** | A custom label to identify this integration (e.g., "Dev GitLab").                                                   |
| **Is Self Hosted**   | Toggle this **on** if you're using **GitLab Self-Managed**.                                                         |

---

### ‚úÖ Step 3: Save and Verify

* Click **Submit Configuration**.
* Codepure will validate your token and establish a secure connection.

---

### üöÄ Step 4: Import Your GitLab Project

After connecting your GitLab account:

1. Go to **Projects**.
2. Click **Import Project**.
3. Choose **GitLab** as your source.
4. Select your repository.
5. Start your security scan.
