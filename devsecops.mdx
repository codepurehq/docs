---
title: 'DevSecOps Integration'
description: 'Learn how Codepure integrates security into your development workflows.'
---

Codepure seamlessly integrates into your CI/CD pipelines, ensuring that security is a part of your development process. It works with tools like GitHub, GitLab, and Jenkins to automate scans and provide actionable results.

Key benefits:

* Supports major CI/CD platforms.

* Automates vulnerability detection during builds.

* Reduces friction between development and security teams.
