Codepure is built from the ground up to help organizations across the Middle East and GCC meet their strictest regulatory requirements.

## Regional Framework Alignment
By keeping your data local and providing deep visibility into your code vulnerabilities, using Codepure helps your organization satisfy the requirements of:
* **Saudi Arabia:** NCA (National Cybersecurity Authority) ECC-1:2024 and SAMA frameworks.
* **United Arab Emirates:** NESA (National Electronic Security Authority) and DESC standards.
* **Qatar:** NCSA (National Cyber Security Agency) NIA policies.
* **Oman & Kuwait:** Local central bank and government data protection mandates.

## Secure by Design
We do not permanently store your source code. Our "scan and discard" architecture ensures that your intellectual property never becomes a liability, simplifying your compliance audits drastically.
