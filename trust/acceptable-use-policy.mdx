Codepure is a powerful cybersecurity tool designed to help organizations find and fix vulnerabilities. To keep our platform safe and legal, everyone using `app.codepure.com` must follow this Acceptable Use Policy (AUP).

## 1. The Golden Rule: Only Scan What You Own
You may only use Codepure to scan source code, APIs, and infrastructure that **you legally own** or have explicit, written permission to test. 

You may not use our platform to scan public repositories, competitor websites, or third-party applications without authorization.

## 2. Prohibited Activities
When using Codepure, you agree that you will **not**:
* **Break the Law:** Use the service for any illegal activities or to violate any local GCC laws.
* **Attack the Platform:** Attempt to hack, overload, or disrupt `app.codepure.com` or our scanning engines.
* **Bypass Billing:** Create multiple free accounts to avoid paying for a premium plan.
* **Share Accounts:** Share your login credentials with unauthorized users outside of your organization.
* **Upload Malicious Code:** Intentionally upload malware, viruses, or destructive code to harm our scanning infrastructure.

## 3. Enforcement
If we discover that a user is violating this policy, we reserve the right to immediately suspend or permanently terminate their account without a refund. We may also report illegal scanning activities to the appropriate cybersecurity authorities.

## 4. Reporting Abuse
If you believe someone is using Codepure to scan your property without your permission, please contact our security team immediately at **fahad@codepure.com**.
