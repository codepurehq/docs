To provide our service, Codepure uses a few trusted third-party companies. 

## 1. Cloud Infrastructure & Hosting
Your source code and scan data are stored locally in the Middle East to meet data sovereignty laws across the GCC.
* **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**: Cloud Hosting & Database.
* **Location:** Middle East Cloud Regions (e.g., Dammam, Saudi Arabia - me-central2).

## 2. Business Operations
We use the following standard services to run our business. These services only handle basic account and billing info, not your source code.
* **Stripe:** Payment Processing & Billing.
* **Intercom:** Customer Support Chat.
* **SendGrid:** Email Notifications.

## A Note on Code Repositories
Tools like GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket are authorized by you. They are not our sub-processors. We only read data from them when you tell us to.
