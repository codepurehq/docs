To provide our service, Codepure uses a few trusted third-party companies[cite: 43]. 

## 1. Cloud Infrastructure & Hosting
Your source code and scan data are stored locally in the GCC to meet data sovereignty laws.
* **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**: Cloud Hosting & Database[cite: 45].
* **Location:** Dammam, Saudi Arabia (me-central2)[cite: 45].

## 2. Business Operations
We use the following standard services to run our business[cite: 46]. These services only handle basic account and billing info, not your source code.
* **Stripe:** Payment Processing & Billing (USA)[cite: 47].
* **Intercom:** Customer Support Chat (USA)[cite: 47].
* **SendGrid:** Email Notifications (USA)[cite: 47].

## A Note on Code Repositories
Tools like GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket are authorized by you. They are not our sub-processors[cite: 50]. We only read data from them when you tell us to[cite: 50].
