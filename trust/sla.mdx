As a crucial part of your DevSecOps pipeline, Codepure is designed for maximum reliability. 

## Uptime Commitment
Codepure guarantees a **99.9% Monthly Uptime Percentage** for our core scanning engines and dashboard. 

## Scheduled Maintenance
We occasionally require maintenance to upgrade our engines. 
* We will notify all administrators at least **48 hours in advance** of any planned downtime.
* Maintenance is always scheduled outside of standard GCC business hours (typically weekends).

## SLA Credits
If we fail to meet our 99.9% uptime guarantee in a given billing month, Enterprise clients are eligible to request a service credit towards their next renewal.
