Codepure is committed to protecting your privacy[cite: 116]. We comply with the **Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)** in Saudi Arabia and the **Federal Decree-Law No. 45** in the UAE[cite: 121, 122].

## 1. Information We Collect
* **From You:** When you sign up, we collect your name, email, username, and organization name[cite: 126]. We also collect billing info, which is handled securely by Stripe[cite: 127].
* **From the Service:** We temporarily access your source code to scan it[cite: 132]. **We do not permanently store your source code.** It is scanned in memory and instantly deleted[cite: 133]. We only save the metadata (e.g., "SQL Injection found on line 45")[cite: 134].

## 2. How We Use Your Info
We use your information to provide the scanning service, generate reports, and keep your account secure[cite: 138, 139]. We never sell your personal data.

## 3. Data Hosting (GCC Region)
To comply with local Saudi and GCC laws, your data is stored securely in the **Google Cloud Dammam Region (me-central2)**[cite: 146]. This ensures compliance with the Saudi National Cybersecurity Authority (NCA)[cite: 147].

## 4. Your Rights
Under Saudi (PDPL) and GCC laws, you have the right to[cite: 162, 163]:
* Know how your data is used[cite: 164].
* View, correct, or update your data[cite: 165, 166].
* Ask us to delete your data when you no longer need our service[cite: 167].

## 5. Security
We protect your data with AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.2+ secure connections, and mandatory Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for our staff[cite: 177, 178].
