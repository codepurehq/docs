# Software Bill of Materials (SBOM)

Codepure automatically generates an SBOM, providing a detailed list of all components in your project. This helps you track dependencies, ensure compliance, and maintain supply chain security.

Key benefits:
- Comprehensive visibility into components.
- Supports compliance with regulations like NIST and ISO 27001.
- Enhances supply chain security by tracking vulnerabilities.
